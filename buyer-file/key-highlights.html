Case Study — 01

Terrace Revitalization

← Drag to compare →
Before After
Transformation Area
27,000 Sq.ft

Unused rooftop terrace — revitalized into a premium revenue-generating guest destination through precision MEP engineering.

The Challenge
01

Ideal Airport-Adjacent Location

Prime site positioning came with strict Aviation Authority regulations that limited the height of any new structure on the terrace.

02

Height Restrictions Due to Airport Authority

Clearance requirements from the Aviation Authority ruled out conventional rooftop construction approaches entirely.

03

Unused 27,000 Sq.ft Terrace

The entire rooftop terrace sat idle, generating zero revenue and delivering no value to guests or the property owner.

04

ADES Cleared Utility Services

All existing MEP utility services had to be fully cleared, rerouted, and rationalised before any transformation work could begin.

The Outcome
01

REVITALIZED TERRACE INTO REVENUE-GENERATING GUEST SPACE

The formerly idle terrace is now a premium hospitality destination, contributing directly to the property's revenue stream.

02

Added Outdoor Lawn for Events

A landscaped open-air lawn now hosts private gatherings, banquets, and corporate events throughout the year.

03

Significantly Increased Client Value

The transformation delivered a measurable uplift in asset valuation, occupancy appeal, and overall guest satisfaction scores.

Case Study — 02

Basement Revitalization

← Drag to compare →
Before After
Transformation Area
3,300 Sq.ft

Former non-revenue spa space — fully reclaimed and converted into a high-value, revenue-generating guest facility.

The Challenge
01

3,300 Sq.ft Occupied by Spa

The basement space was occupied by a non-revenue spa facility, leaving thousands of prime square feet entirely underutilised.

02

Outdated MEP Infrastructure

Ageing plumbing, electrical, and mechanical systems from the original spa fit-out had to be fully stripped, documented, and rationalised.

03

Basement Spatial Constraints

Restricted ceiling heights and limited access routes made the MEP re-engineering a precise, complex coordination challenge.

The Outcome
01

Revitalized Basement

The space has been fully transformed into a functional, operational guest area that now generates direct revenue for the property.

02

Reclaiming 3,300 Sq.ft for Productive Use

Every square foot of the former spa was repurposed into high-value guest-facing space through intelligent MEP redesign and spatial planning.

03

Functional Space, Generating Revenue

A space once classified as a cost centre has been re-engineered into a revenue asset — demonstrating ADES's ability to unlock value at every level of a building.
