Project Process Flow

How we deliver excellence from concept to commissioning

Step 01

Building Survey & Feasibility Study

Comprehensive site assessment and viability analysis to establish project foundations.

Step 02

Concept Design Submittal

Design concepts developed based on complete client input and requirements.

Step 03

Budgetary Proposal

Cost estimation and financial planning based on approved concept design.

Step 04

Detail Design Development

Engineering-driven detailed design development for all MEPF systems.

Step 05

Bill of Quantities

Preparation of comprehensive BOQ for accurate project costing.

Step 06

Material & Technical Specifications

Detailed specifications for materials, equipment, and installation standards.

Step 07

Tender Documents & Floating

Preparation of tender documents and release to qualified contractors.

Step 08

Vendor Bid Evaluation

Review and evaluation of bids to select the right contractor for execution.

Step 09

Construction Documents

Contractor-prepared construction drawings and execution plans.

Step 10

Commissioning & Execution Analysis

Pre and post commissioning with performance analysis of installed systems.
